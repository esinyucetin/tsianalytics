## Why TSI?

Traditional methods focus only on the "magnitude" (amplitude) of data, while TSI reads the data as a geometric trajectory. Even if two systems produce similar values, they can diverge behaviorally. We measure this directional consistency.

Core Advantages of TSI

* **Speed & Scalability:** Operates in real-time at $O(n)$ complexity without bottlenecking your systems, unlike heavy Dynamic Time Warping (DTW) algorithms.
    
* **Natural Noise Filter:** Mathematically minimizes noise variance by 4x using a secant-based geometric architecture, without applying destructive low-pass filters to your raw data.

* **Early Warning System (Event Horizon):** Predicts the exact moment a single signal will change regimes before the crash happens, without needing an external reference signal.
